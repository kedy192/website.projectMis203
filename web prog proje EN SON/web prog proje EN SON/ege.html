Black-Eyed Pea Salad (Börülce Salatası)

A light and refreshing salad, very common in the Aegean region, usually served cold as a meze.

Traditional Black-Eyed Pea Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black-eyed peas
  • Tomatoes, diced
  • Cucumber, diced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Parsley and dill
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Optional: White cheese (feta-style)

Instructions

  1. Soak the black-eyed peas if needed, then boil until soft but not mushy. Drain and let cool.
  2. Chop tomatoes, cucumber, red onion and herbs.
  3. Place the peas and vegetables in a bowl.
  4. Add olive oil, lemon juice and salt.
  5. Mix gently and add crumbled white cheese on top if you are using it.
  6. Serve cold as a meze or light main dish.

Vegan Black-Eyed Pea Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black-eyed peas
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion

  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the peas and drain.

  3. Add lemon, olive oil, and salt.
  4. Serve cold. You can add fresh herbs like parsley for more flavour.

Imam Bayıldı (Stuffed Eggplant)

A famous Aegean dish made with eggplants stuffed with onion, tomato and garlic, cooked in plenty of olive oil.

Traditional Imam Bayıldı

Ingredients

  • 4 eggplants
  • 3 onions, sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4–5 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil (or more, traditionally)
  • Salt, black pepper
  • A pinch of sugar
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Peel eggplants in stripes and soak in salted water for about 15 minutes.
  2. Dry them and lightly fry or bake until they soften.
  3. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  4. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper and a pinch of sugar, cook for a few minutes.
  5. Place eggplants in a baking dish and slit the centre to open a pocket.
  6. Fill with the onion–tomato mixture.
  7. Add a splash of water to the dish and drizzle with extra olive oil.
  8. Bake at medium heat for about 25–30 minutes.
  9. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Vegan Imam Bayıldı

Ingredients

  • 4 eggplants
  • 3 onions
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 4–5 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt, a pinch of sugar

Instructions

  1. Peel eggplants in stripes, slit the center, and bake or lightly fry.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic, add tomatoes.
  3. Fill eggplants with this mixture.
  4. Add a splash of water + a pinch of sugar.
  5. Cook 25–30 minutes on low heat. Serve as a cold olive oil dish.

Stuffed Grape Leaves (Yaprak Sarma / Dolma)

A classic olive oil dish, often served at special occasions with pine nuts and currants.

Traditional Stuffed Grape Leaves (with Olive Oil)

Ingredients (Filling)

  • 1.5 cups rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 2 tbsp currants
  • 1 tbsp allspice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp dried mint
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Grape leaves (pickled or fresh blanched)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and pine nuts in olive oil until lightly golden.
  2. Add rice and fry for a few minutes.
  3. Add currants, spices and salt, mix well and add a little hot water.
  4. Cook the filling briefly (the rice will finish cooking inside the leaves).
  5. Place a small amount of filling on each grape leaf and roll tightly.
  6. Arrange the rolls tightly in a pot.
  7. Add water, extra olive oil and a little salt on top.
  8. Place a plate over the dolmas to keep them in place and cook on low heat for 40–50 minutes.
  9. Let them cool in the pot and serve at room temperature.

Vegan Stuffed Grape Leaves

Ingredients (Filling)

  • 1.5 cups rice
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp pine nuts
  • 2 tbsp currants
  • 1 tbsp allspice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp dried mint
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Grape leaves

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion + pine nuts.
  2. Add rice, currants, spices, salt; cook lightly.
  3. Wrap mixture inside grape leaves.
  4. Place tightly in a pot, add water + olive oil.
  5. Cook 40–50 minutes on low heat.

Şakşuka (Aegean Vegetable Meze)

A colourful dish made with fried or baked vegetables in a tomato and garlic sauce, served as a cold meze.

Traditional Şakşuka

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2 green peppers
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil or frying oil
  • Salt
  • Optional: yogurt for serving

Instructions

  1. Cut eggplants, zucchinis and peppers into cubes.
  2. Fry the vegetables in hot oil until golden, or bake them with a little oil in the oven.
  3. For the sauce, cook chopped tomatoes and minced garlic in olive oil until thickened, then season with salt.
  4. Mix the vegetables with the tomato sauce or pour the sauce on top.
  5. Serve cold, optionally with yogurt on the side.

Vegan Şakşuka (Aegean Style)

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2 peppers
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cut vegetables into cubes and bake or lightly fry.
  2. Cook tomatoes + garlic into a sauce.
  3. Mix vegetables with the sauce.
  4. Serve cold or at room temperature.
