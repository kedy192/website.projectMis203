Mantı

A classic Central Anatolian dumpling dish served with garlic yogurt and butter sauce.

Traditional Mantı

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 200 g ground beef
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • Salt, pepper
  • Yogurt (for topping)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • Dried mint, paprika

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with flour, water, egg, and salt; let it rest.
  2. Mix beef, onion, salt, and pepper for filling.
  3. Roll dough thin, cut squares, place filling, fold into mantı shapes.
  4. Boil in salted water until cooked.
  5. Serve with garlic yogurt and butter–tomato paste sauce; sprinkle mint and paprika.

Vegan Mantı

Ingredients

  • Dough without egg (flour + water + salt)
  • Sautéed mushrooms + soy mince
  • Soy or almond yogurt
  • Olive oil
  • Dried mint, paprika

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough (no egg) and roll thin.
  2. Fill with mushroom–soy mixture.
  3. Boil until soft.
  4. Serve with vegan yogurt and olive oil–mint sauce.

Konya Tandır Çorbası

A warming yogurty soup with meat, bulgur, and chickpeas from Konya.

Traditional Konya Tandır Soup

Ingredients

  • 300 g diced lamb or beef
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup bulgur
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 5 cups water or meat stock
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt, pepper, dried mint

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat in water or stock until tender.
  2. Add bulgur and chickpeas.
  3. Whisk yogurt with egg yolk; temper with hot soup.
  4. Add to the pot and simmer gently.
  5. Serve with melted butter and mint.

Vegan Tandır Soup

Ingredients

  • Tofu or mushrooms (instead of meat)
  • Cooked chickpeas
  • Bulgur
  • Soy or cashew yogurt
  • Vegetable stock
  • Olive oil
  • Dried mint

Instructions

  1. Sauté tofu or mushrooms lightly.
  2. Add bulgur, chickpeas, and vegetable stock.
  3. Temper vegan yogurt with hot broth.
  4. Add, simmer, and serve with mint.

Kayseri Yağlaması

Layered flatbread topped with a rich meat sauce, served with yogurt.

Traditional Kayseri Yağlaması

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200 g ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp pepper paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, pepper, paprika
  • Garlic yogurt (topping)

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough and roll thin layers (şebit).
  2. Cook beef filling with spices and paste.
  3. Layer bread and filling; bake briefly.
  4. Serve with garlic yogurt.

Vegan Kayseri Yağlaması

Ingredients

  • Dough (flour, water, yeast, salt)
  • Soy mince + crushed walnuts
  • Onion, tomato, pepper
  • Tomato + pepper paste
  • Olive oil
  • Soy yogurt

Instructions

  1. Roll dough into layers.
  2. Cook soy-walnut filling.
  3. Layer and bake lightly.
  4. Serve with vegan yogurt.

Ankara Tava

A famous Ankara dish with rice and sautéed meat, cooked in one pot.

Traditional Ankara Tava

Ingredients

  • 500 g cubed lamb or beef
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 cups stock
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Sauté meat in butter or oil.
  2. Add chopped vegetables.
  3. Add rice and broth; simmer until cooked.
  4. Serve hot.

Vegan Ankara Tava

Ingredients

  • Roasted chickpeas + mushrooms
  • Rice
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetables (onion, tomato, pepper)
  • Vegetable broth
  • Optional: roasted nuts

Instructions

  1. Sauté mushrooms and chickpeas.
  2. Add vegetables.
  3. Add rice and vegetable stock; cook.
  4. Top with roasted nuts.

Nevzine Dessert

A traditional tahini–walnut dessert from Kayseri, soaked in syrup.

Traditional Nevzine Dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup tahini
  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Syrup

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients into dough, add walnuts.
  2. Shape and bake at 180°C until golden.
  3. Pour syrup over hot dessert.

Vegan Nevzine Dessert

Ingredients

  • Soy or almond yogurt
  • Coconut oil or vegan butter
  • Flour, sugar, tahini, walnuts
  • Baking powder
  • Syrup ingredients (already vegan)

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with vegan yogurt & oil.
  2. Bake until golden.
  3. Add syrup while hot.
