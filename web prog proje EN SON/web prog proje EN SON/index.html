Vegan Flavors of Turkey

This website mainly presents vegan versions of regional Turkish cuisine.

Today, vegan diet had been becoming much more popular due to some factors from social media effects to increased environmental awareness and sensitivity.

Vegan diets are beneficial in terms of high fiber consumption. Moreover, it helps to reduce the risks for chronic diseases. Carbon footprint ve greenhouse gas emissions are minimized thanks to vegan diets.

This webpage aims to show the Turkish popular recipes region by region, and guides users to divert the popular tastes into vegan recipes without disrupting their original flavor.

The user can reach the recipes towards buttons on the map, and enjoy their meals!
